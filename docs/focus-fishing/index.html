    🎣 How to Fish

    1
    Pick a sinker once on the setup screen — it sticks for the whole session. Heavier = deeper water = bigger fish, but much harder to reel in!
    2
    Cast your line — click/tap when the power bar hits the green zone for a long, accurate cast. Farther = rarer fish, but longer wait.
    3
    Wait for a bite — watch the bobber. Deep casts take longer. Rainy/dawn weather means fish bite faster!
    4
    3-2-1 GET READY — you have 3 seconds to prepare before the fight starts!
    5
    Reel it in! — Hold to pull the dot right. Release to ease tension. Keep the dot in the GREEN zone to drain the fish's stamina.
    6
    Watch for surges! — the fish will fight hard. React fast! If the dot leaves green for more than 1.5 seconds, it escapes!
