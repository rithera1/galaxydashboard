    🎣 How to Fish

    1
    Choose a Sinker — Heavier sinkers reach deeper water with bigger, rarer fish. But harder to reel in!
    2
    Cast Your Line — Click/tap when the power bar hits the green zone for a long, accurate cast.
    3
    Wait for a Bite — Watch the bobber bob. Patience pays off!
    4
    Reel It In! — Hold the button/key to reel. Release to ease tension. Keep the dot in the GREEN zone to tire the fish out!
    5
    Watch out for Surges! — The fish will fight hard sometimes. React fast or it'll escape!
    6
    Don't lose it! — If the dot leaves the green, you have 1.5 seconds to bring it back. After that — the fish wins!
