# GalaxyDashboard

A collection of educational mini-apps for kids, served via GitHub Pages.

**Home:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/)

---

## 🐝 Spelling Bee
A spelling and vocabulary practice app.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/bee-studio](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/bee-studio/)

- Hear a word read aloud and type the spelling
- Match words to their definitions
- Admin panel (PIN-protected) to add and manage word lists
- Backed by Firebase Firestore

## 📐 Perimeter Pals
A math learning game focused on perimeter, shapes, and measurement.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/perimeter-pals](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/perimeter-pals/)

- Learn mode with step-by-step geometry lessons
- Practice challenges with XP and streak tracking
- Number line, shape tools, and estimation activities

## ☄️ Asteroid Addition
A space arcade game where you blast asteroids by solving addition problems.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/asteroid-add](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/asteroid-add/)

- Waves of asteroids carrying math problems
- Shoot the correct answer before they reach you
- Difficulty scales as you progress

## 🌌 Solar System Explorer
An interactive solar system with tap-to-explore facts about every planet and moon.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/solar-system](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/solar-system/)

- Animated orbiting planets, moons, asteroid belt, and comet
- Zoom in on any object to read fact cards (shuffled each visit)
- Swipe left/right through facts; swipe down to peek the panel, swipe up to restore
- Minimap shows your current position as you pan and zoom
- "Show to scale" view for a true-size planet comparison
- Mouse wheel + drag on desktop; pinch-to-zoom + touch pan on mobile

## 🌍 Geo Guesser
A geography quiz — click the map to place where you think the location is.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/geo-guesser](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/geo-guesser/)

- Three modes: US States, US Cities, Countries of the World
- Leaflet tile maps with animated guess lines and score-based feedback
- Score based on distance from correct answer, with streak bonuses
- Progress dots, timer bar, and end-of-game summary
- Configurable round count (5 / 10 / 15)

## ⚖️ Planet Weight
An interactive tool that shows how much you'd weigh (and how you'd move) on every world in the solar system.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/planet-weight](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/planet-weight/)

- Enter your weight in lbs or kg and explore all 11 worlds (Mercury → Sun + Moon + Pluto)
- Animated jump-height, running-speed, and throw-distance comparisons for each planet
- Rich fact panel for every world: temperature, atmosphere, survival time, day length, moons, diameter, distance from Sun, and two fun facts
- Color-coded gravity bar showing each planet's pull relative to the Sun
- Responsive planet grid with 3-D sphere rendering and Saturn/Uranus rings

## ⏳ Timeline Jumper
A drag-and-drop history game — place events on the correct year before checking your answers.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/timeline-jumper](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/timeline-jumper/)

- Three modes with 8 rounds each (3 randomly selected per play-through for variety):
  - 🇺🇸 **US History** — Colonial Era, Civil War, Modern America, Progressive Era, Space Age, World War Era, Revolution & Reform, Technology & Innovation
  - 🌍 **World History** — Medieval, Age of Revolutions, World at War, Age of Empire, Post-War, Ancient Civilizations, Renaissance & Exploration, 20th Century Turning Points
  - 🔬 **Science** — Scientific Revolution, Industrial Age, Modern Science, 20th Century Breakthroughs, Digital Age, Ancient & Medieval Science, Medicine & Biology, Space & Computing
- Drag event cards onto matching year slots; 100 pts for exact match, 25 pts for one slot off
- Wikipedia thumbnail images auto-loaded for each event card
- Warp-burst drop animation and correct/wrong glow feedback

## 💬 Speech Blaster
A fast-paced parts-of-speech arcade game — defend your planet by blasting the right words out of the sky.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/speech-blaster](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/speech-blaster/)

- Three difficulty levels: Easy (2 types, slow), Medium (all 3 types), Hard (fast chaos)
- 5 rounds per game, each targeting a different part of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives)
- **Instant laser** — clicking fires a bright instant beam with glow, core, and expanding blast rings (no slow projectile)
- **Between-round upgrades** — after every round, pick one of three random upgrades:
  - 🔫 Double Blaster — two barrels fire at once (visual)
  - 🔥 Plasma Cannon / ❄️ Cryo Beam / 🌈 Rainbow Laser / 💪 Power Beam — laser color & style changes
  - 🛡️ Planet Shield — blocks your next mistake
  - ❤️ Extra Life — +1 heart
  - ⭐ Star Bonus — +50 points instantly
  - 📡 Wide Beam — larger click hit area
- **Escalating points** — each round's base score is 20% higher than the previous
- ✨ Glowing special words grant bonus powerups (extra life, time slow, 2× multiplier)
- 80+ words per category; point multiplier, shield, and slow-time powerup system
