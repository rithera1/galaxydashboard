# GalaxyDashboard

A collection of educational mini-apps for kids, served via GitHub Pages.

**Home:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/)

---

## 🐝 Spelling Bee
A spelling and vocabulary practice app.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/bee-studio](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/bee-studio/)

- Hear a word read aloud and type the spelling
- Match words to their definitions
- Admin panel (PIN-protected) to add and manage word lists
- Backed by Firebase Firestore

---

## 📐 Perimeter Pals
A math learning game focused on perimeter, shapes, and measurement.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/perimeter-pals](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/perimeter-pals/)

- Learn mode with step-by-step geometry lessons
- Practice challenges with XP and streak tracking
- Number line, shape tools, and estimation activities

---

## ☄️ Asteroid Addition
A space arcade game where you blast asteroids by solving addition problems.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/asteroid-add](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/asteroid-add/)

- Waves of asteroids carrying math problems
- Shoot the correct answer before they reach you
- Difficulty scales as you progress

---

## 🌌 Solar System Explorer
An interactive solar system with tap-to-explore facts about every planet and moon.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/solar-system](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/solar-system/)

- Animated orbiting planets, moons, asteroid belt, and comet
- Zoom in on any object to read fact cards (shuffled each visit)
- Swipe left/right through facts; swipe down to peek the panel, swipe up to restore
- Minimap shows your current position as you pan and zoom
- "Show to scale" view for a true-size planet comparison
- Mouse wheel + drag on desktop; pinch-to-zoom + touch pan on mobile

---

## 🌍 Geo Guesser
A geography quiz — click the map to place where you think the location is.

**Live:** [rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/geo-guesser](https://rithera1.github.io/galaxydashboard/geo-guesser/)

- Three modes: US States, US Cities, Countries of the World
- Leaflet tile maps with animated guess lines and score-based feedback
- Score based on distance from correct answer, with streak bonuses
- Progress dots, timer bar, and end-of-game summary
- Configurable round count (5 / 10 / 15)
